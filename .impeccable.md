## Design Context

### Users

LoveACE serves students who need to identify and download the correct native
client quickly. The download page should reduce platform and artifact ambiguity
while making version and integrity information easy to verify.

### Brand Personality

Approachable, trustworthy, direct.

### Aesthetic Direction

Preserve the existing light-blue identity and student-friendly tone. The v2
download page uses the cat-and-fish riverbank illustration as a fixed backdrop,
with lake blue, olive green, warm yellow, and ink gray drawn from the artwork.
Support complete system light and dark themes. Avoid generic glassmorphism,
neon gradients, excessive cards, and ornamental technical styling.

### Design Principles

- Put the detected platform and correct official artifact first.
- Keep the illustration recognizable without sacrificing text contrast.
- Use one clear information column and progressive disclosure for checksums.
- Treat version, build, file size, and SHA-256 as trust signals.
- Preserve all critical actions and information on mobile and with reduced motion.
